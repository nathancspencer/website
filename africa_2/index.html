Africa Part 2: A lot with a little

April 2025

Some people have asked me how this trip has changed me, or the way I live my life. I wasn't sure.

But I think I know now. Material pursuits have lost their meaning to me. We all live in the mansions and the current topical election is housing affordability. But having seen people content with so little, it is quite striking how little you need to live a life. And not only that, it was a chance for myself to live a life with so little possessions and things to do.

I feel that city life can maybe distract the soul, with so many activities!
